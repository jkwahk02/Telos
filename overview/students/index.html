How to request a meeting
1
Go to the Meetings tab
You'll see advisor cards for each of your assigned advisors (Primary and Secondary). Each card shows whether the advisor currently has available time slots.
2
Click "Request meeting"
A request form will open. Available time slots for the next two weeks will be listed — select one or more that work for you, giving your advisor options to choose from.
3
Add an agenda note
Briefly describe what you'd like to discuss. This helps your advisor prepare and makes the meeting more productive. Then click Submit request.
4
Wait for confirmation
Your advisor will review the request and confirm one of your selected slots. You'll see the confirmed time and meeting link appear in your Overview and Meetings tab.
5
Write a reflection after the meeting
Once the meeting is marked completed, go to Meetings → history and add a reflection and key takeaway. This builds your own record of your advising journey.

Key features
Meeting requests
Pick from your advisor's available slots and submit a request with an agenda. Your advisor confirms one slot — you see the update in real time.
Profile & goals
Build your advising profile with career goals, research interests, and strengths. Your advisor can see this before each meeting.
Meeting history
See a chronological record of all your past advising sessions — what was discussed, action items, and your personal reflections.
Reflections
After each meeting, write a brief reflection and key takeaway. These are private to you and build your personal advising record over time.
Reschedule & withdraw
Need to change a pending request? Propose a new time or withdraw the request entirely from the Meetings tab before your advisor confirms.
Secure login
Sign in with your @taejae.ac.kr student email. You can only see your own advising data — not other students' records.
