How each platform works
Faculty — logging a session
1
Go to My Students and click a student's row to expand their timeline.
2
Click + Log session — student name, ID, and your advisor role are auto-filled.
3
Fill in the Discussion summary (required) and optionally add student concerns, advice, and next meeting date.
4
Click Save — the record appears instantly in the timeline and Session Log.
Students — requesting a meeting
1
Go to Meetings and find your advisor's card with available time slots.
2
Select one or more preferred slots and submit your request with an agenda note.
3
Your advisor reviews and confirms one slot — you receive the meeting link.
4
After the meeting, write a reflection and key takeaway in your meeting history.
Key features
Faculty
Chair dashboard
Program Chair sees an aggregated view across all advisors — flagging students with zero sessions, unscheduled meetings, or pending follow-ups.
Faculty
Two-pane session log
Each session records discussion notes and student concerns in separate colored panes, plus action items, follow-up status, and next meeting date.
Faculty
Student roster view
Filter by your advisees, search by name or ID, sort by session count or meeting date, and expand any student for their full advising timeline.
Students
Profile & goals
Students build a profile with career goals, research interests, and strengths. A completion checklist on the Overview nudges them to fill it out.
Students
Meeting requests
Students pick from faculty's published availability windows. Advisors confirm one slot — status updates in real time on both sides.
Both
Secure authentication
Taejae email-based login via Supabase Auth. Forced password change on first login. Each session is scoped to the signed-in user.
