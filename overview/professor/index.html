How to log an advising session
1
Open the student's timeline
Go to My Students and click any row to expand the student's advising history. You'll see all past sessions and the next scheduled meeting.
2
Click "+ Log session"
The button appears in the top-right of the expanded panel. The student's name, ID, and your advisor role (Primary / Secondary) are pre-filled automatically.
3
Fill in session details
Set the date, type (Regular / Ad-hoc / Email), and format (Zoom / In-person / Phone). Then fill in the two main content fields:

Discussion summary — what you discussed from your perspective. Required.
Student concerns & requests — what the student raised or expressed. Optional but recommended.
4
Add follow-up and next meeting
Note any action items (yours and the student's). Set Follow-up required to Yes if you need to act before the next session. If the next meeting is already arranged, enter the date and agenda.
5
Save
Click Save session. The record is saved to the database immediately and appears in both the student's timeline and Session Log.

Key features
Chair dashboard
If you are Program Chair, the Overview shows an aggregated view across all advisors — flagging at-risk students program-wide.
Two-pane session log
Blue pane for your discussion notes, amber pane for student concerns — kept separate for clarity and continuity across sessions.
Roster & search
Filter to your advisees, search by name or student ID, sort by session count, last meeting, or next meeting date.
Edit & delete sessions
Correct a saved entry from Session Log at any time. Deletion requires confirmation and cannot be undone.
Availability & requests
Publish your weekly availability slots in Settings. Students request from those windows; you confirm one slot per request.
Secure login
Sign in with your @taejae.ac.kr faculty email. First-login password change is enforced before accessing any advising data.
